Important Browser Information

Here are some things you should be aware of when using a web browser:

  • Privacy Risks: Websites can track your browsing activity using cookies and other technologies.
  • Security Threats: You may encounter malware, phishing attempts, and other security threats.
  • Information Collection: Browsers collect data about your browsing habits, which may be used for personalization or advertising.
  • Incognito Mode: Incognito mode helps protect your privacy on the current device, but it does not make you invisible online.
